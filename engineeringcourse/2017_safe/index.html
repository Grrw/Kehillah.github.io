      What is the project? Parts Purpose How it works
      Our project is a safe designed to be seethrough, yet secure.
      		To make this box we needed 2 sheets of 30x36' plexiglass, 8 blocks of 2.5 inch thick and 8 inch long wood, and a 3d printer with plastic.
      • Provides reasonable security to your items
      • It's see through, Impress your friends!'
      		After plugging the arduino chip into power, the two RFID keycards act as a small puzzle. In order to properly encode the safe, you must touch one card to the reader, followed by the other, and then do it again. If you are succesful the door opens, as the reader sends a signal to the small servo motor controlling the gear of the lock.
